Forest Honey


Forest Honey is one of the most profound offerings from The Honey Shop. This Honey is collected from wild bees found in deep forests. The value of honey depends upon the flowers from which bees collect their nectar.

Liquid honey


Liquid honey is basically honeys natural form: when produced by the bees and stored in the comb in the hive, honey is in a liquid state. Aside from being filtered and pasteurized, Bee Maid's liquid honey you can buy on the shelves is the same honey that you find in the hives.

Creamed Honey


Creamed honey is honey that has been processed to control crystallization. Also known as honey fondant, it has a smooth, spreadable consistency and lighter color than liquid honey of the same floral type.
